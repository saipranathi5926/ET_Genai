# MyET AI: Business Impact Model

## Executive Summary
The integration of a fully autonomous multi-agent news analysis pipeline into The Economic Times digital footprint is projected to drive substantial engagement compounding, reduce operational bottlenecks in multimedia localization, and uncover blocked subscription revenue.

## 1. Core Assumptions
*   **Active Monthly Subscriber Base (ET Prime):** ~1,000,000 users.
*   **Average Read Time per User:** 4 mins/day.
*   **Average Cost of Manual Video Production (News Summary Clip):** $150 per synthesized clip.
*   **Average Translation Cost (Per Article, Regional Vernacular):** $15.
*   **Click-Through Rate (CTR) via Standard Algorithms:** 1.8%.

---

## 2. Quantified Business Impact

### A. Cost Reductions (AI Video Studio & Vernacular Engine)
Currently, transforming written business news into broadcast-quality short-form video or culturally adapted vernacular scripts requires heavy human intervention.

*   **Video Production:** 
    *   *Old Way:* Producing 20 breaking news summary videos daily costs ~$3,000/day.
    *   *New Way:* Browser-native TTS and agentic templating reduces this marginal cost to near $0. 
    *   **Impact:** ~$1.09 Million operational cost saved annually.
*   **Vernacular Localization:** 
    *   *Old Way:* Manual translation of top 100 key articles into 4 languages costs ~$6,000/day.
    *   *New Way:* Agentic contextual translation reduces the marginal cost to API token usage (~$0.02 per article).
    *   **Impact:** ~99% margin expansion on regional content delivery.

### B. Revenue Recovered / Grown (Hyper-Personalization & NewsNavigator)
The "Persona Aligner" matches specific underlying financial instruments with logged-in user demographics.
*   By increasing the relevance of the feed, we project a transition of CTR from 1.8% to a conservative **3.5%**.
*   **Back-of-Envelope Math:** 
    *   1M users reading 4 articles/day = 4M impressions.
    *   CTR jump from 1.8% (72k clicks) to 3.5% (140k clicks).
    *   An additional 68,000 highly-engaged daily clicks.
    *   Assuming a 0.5% conversion rate on the "Subscribe to Bookmark" CTA embedded explicitly on highly targeted articles:
    *   68,000 clicks * 0.005 conversion * $30/yr subscription = **$10,200 in recovered net-new subscription revenue generated every single day** ($3.72M annually).

### C. Time Saved (Reader Side)
*   **NewsNavigator Briefings:** Users currently spend ~12 minutes piecing together 6-8 disjointed articles regarding a developing story (e.g., Union Budget). 
*   The Navigator synthesizes this into a 90-second explorable read.
*   **Impact:** ~10.5 minutes saved per active research session, cementing MyET AI as an absolutely vital, irreplaceable daily productivity tool rather than just a passive news site.
